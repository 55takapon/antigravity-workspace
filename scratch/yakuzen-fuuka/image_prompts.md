# Image Generation Prompts for Yakuzen "Fuuka" (Refined)

Based on deep research into Japanese Yakuzen aesthetics, these prompts emphasize "Ishoku-dogen" (healing through food), seasonal ingredients, and a sophisticated, earthen atmosphere.

## 1. Hero Image (First View)
**Concept:** A majestic, steaming Donabe (earthenware pot) centerpiece, conveying warmth, healing, and the richness of medicinal ingredients. The atmosphere should be "dimly lit luxury" (shadow and light).

*   **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
*   **Keywords:** Donabe, Steam, Wolfberries, Dates, Moody, Earthenware.
*   **Prompt:**
    > Cinematic food photography of a Japanese Yakuzen hot pot (Nabe) served in a rustic black clay Donabe pot, sitting on a dark walnut table. The broth is rich and golden amber. Visible ingredients: distinct red wolfberries (Goji), whole jujube dates, lotus root slices, wood ear mushrooms, and fresh mizuna greens. Gentle steam rising in shafts of warm natural light. Background is a blurred traditional Japanese interior with shoji screens. Mood is healing, serene, and sophisticated. 8k resolution, highly detailed texture, chiaroscuro lighting.

## 2. Concept Image (Philosophy)
**Concept:** "The Pharmacist's Table" - A curated display of raw medicinal ingredients, showing the source of the power. Beauty in dryness and texture.

*   **Aspect Ratio:** 4:3
*   **Keywords:** Medicinal herbs, Dried ingredients, Ceramic, Wooden texture.
*   **Prompt:**
    > Artistic still life photography of traditional Japanese medicinal herbs arranged on a weathered wooden board. Ingredients include dried red dates, star anise, cinnamon sticks, dried chrysanthemum flowers, and white lotus seeds. A small, handmade ceramic cup of herbal tea sits nearby with a wisp of steam. Soft, diffused window light from the side (Rembrandt lighting). Colors are earthy browns, deep reds, and muted golds. Minimalist "Wabi-sabi" aesthetic. Macro shot, shallow depth of field.

## 3. Menu Images
**Concept:** Refined, appetizing, and healthy. Emphasizing the "set" nature of Japanese dining (Ichiju-Sansai).

### A. Seasonal Course (Kaiseki)
*   **Aspect Ratio:** 3:2
*   **Prompt:**
    > High-angle shot of a modern Yakuzen Kaiseki course. Small, exquisite portions served on handmade Japanese ceramics of varying shapes and earth tones. Includes a small bowl of medicinal soup with floating wolfberries, grilled seasonal fish, and a side of vegetable dish. The arrangement is slightly asymmetrical (Fukinsei). Soft, ambient lighting, elegant and high-class atmosphere.

### B. Yakuzen Hot Pot (Nabe - Focus)
*   **Aspect Ratio:** 3:2
*   **Prompt:**
    > Close-up, 45-degree angle shot of a personal-sized Yakuzen hot pot using a "Shabu-shabu" style presentation. Thinly sliced high-quality pork or beef arranged next to a basket of vibrant medicinal vegetables (enoki mushrooms, garland chrysanthemum, carrots). The broth in the pot is bubbling gently. Focus on the freshness of the ingredients and the richness of the soup. Professional culinary magazine style.

### C. Lunch Set (Gozen)
*   **Aspect Ratio:** 3:2
*   **Prompt:**
    > A healthy "Yakuzen Gozen" lunch tray viewed from above. Wooden tray containing brown rice (or mixed grain rice), a main dish of steamed chicken with herbal sauce, delicate pickles, and miso soup. Natural sunlight casting soft shadows of leaves (komorebi) on the table, suggesting a peaceful lunch break. Fresh, organic, and wholesome vibe.

## 4. Map Image (Access)

*   **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or 4:3
